# Do Not Restore Paper-Stage Literature Search

Restoring the full PaperOrchestra Plotting Agent does not restore upstream online literature search. PaperOrchestra may extract, organize, deduplicate, format, and cite only literature evidence already present in the Research Draft or authoritative Discovery artifacts. When support is absent, it narrows or omits the claim rather than initiating research or inserting an unverified citation; citation coverage remains a Discovery research responsibility.
