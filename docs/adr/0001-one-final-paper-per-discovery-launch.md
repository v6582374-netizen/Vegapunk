# One Final Paper per Discovery Launch

Vegapunk will produce at most one Final Paper for a Discovery Launch, centered on one Selected Paper Candidate. An explicit candidate choice takes precedence; automatic selection is allowed only when the primary metric and optimization direction make successful candidates reliably comparable and identify a unique best result. Ambiguous or tied launches enter Awaiting Candidate Selection instead of silently guessing. This boundary preserves a coherent scientific narrative and PaperOrchestra's single-paper input model while avoiding uncontrolled per-candidate paper generation or an implicitly broader comparison study.

**Considered Options**

- Generate one paper for every successful Candidate Experiment. Rejected because it multiplies cost and produces competing manuscripts from one research effort.
- Combine multiple Candidate Experiments into a comparison paper. Rejected because it requires a different evidence and narrative model and is outside the minimal migration boundary.
