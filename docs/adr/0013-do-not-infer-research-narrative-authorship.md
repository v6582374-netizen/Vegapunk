# Do Not Infer Research Narrative Authorship

The initial Research Narrative title will be taken directly from the Selected Research Candidate's executed method title, and PaperOrchestra may not invent or replace it. Author names, institutions, and contact information remain empty unless supplied later through explicit configuration. InternAgent project contributors, operating-system users, Git identities, and agent names are not treated as scientific authors, because repository or execution identity is not evidence of authorship.

The document date is the recorded Dossier Run generation date. Empty authorship metadata must remain valid input to the ElegantPaper template and must not be filled by a writing or refinement agent.

**Considered Options**

- Attribute the narrative to InternAgent or one of its agents. Rejected because generation attribution is not scientific authorship.
- Infer authors from repository metadata or the local environment. Rejected because those identities do not establish responsibility for the research claims.
