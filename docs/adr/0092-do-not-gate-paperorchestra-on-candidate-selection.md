# Do Not Gate PaperOrchestra on Candidate Selection

Draft Handoff always starts PaperOrchestra after the configured Discovery work reaches its handoff point. A Terminal Candidate Selection, when available, is supplied as preferred scientific context; it is not required to start or resume a PaperOrchestra Run. Without a selection, PaperOrchestra constructs the strongest paper supported by the Research Draft and available authoritative artifacts, including negative or incomplete findings where appropriate, without inventing missing evidence.
