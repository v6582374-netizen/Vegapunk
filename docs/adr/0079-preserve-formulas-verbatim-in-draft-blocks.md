# Preserve Formulas Verbatim in Draft Blocks

Every observable formula belongs in the Research Draft as source material, not as an optional presentation detail. Capture preserves the original representation and context, including LaTeX, MathML, plain-text notation, inline/display delimiters, variable definitions, assumptions, and derivation steps; a formula that appears only inside a binary artifact is referenced by its artifact metadata rather than invented by the capture layer. No hook may summarize, normalize, or discard mathematical content.
